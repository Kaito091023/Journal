PURPOSE


The Purpose of our Advocacy


The purpose of our advocacy, “Empowering Society Through Responsible Use of ICT and Social Media,” is to guide people—especially the youth—toward becoming responsible, respectful, and aware users of technology. We want to remind everyone that social media and ICT are not just tools for entertainment, but powerful platforms that can shape opinions, influence behavior, and create change. Our advocacy exists to promote mindfulness in the digital world, helping individuals understand the importance of using technology in ways that build trust, spread truth, and encourage kindness.


We believe that by promoting responsible digital behavior, we can reduce the negative effects of the internet such as cyberbullying, misinformation, and online hate. This advocacy gives people the knowledge and awareness they need to use technology safely and positively—turning the online world into a space of learning, respect, and empowerment.


The Goals of our Advocacy


  1. To promote digital literacy and awareness
    –     Help individuals understand how ICT and social media work, and how to use them wisely.

  2. To encourage responsible online behavior
    –     Inspire people to think before posting, respect others online, and protect their digital privacy.

  3. To fight misinformation and cyberbullying
    –     Spread awareness about the effects of fake news, online harassment, and how to prevent them.

  4. To build a positive online community
    –     Create a supportive digital space where kindness, truth, and empathy are valued.

  5. To empower the youth as digital citizens
    –     Equip young people with the knowledge and confidence to use ICT as a tool for learning, advocacy, and social change.
